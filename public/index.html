diyrrr (pronounced dear)

diyrrr is a call to action, a space where hacking, making, and listening become acts of resistance and reimagination. The name stands for 'Do It Yourself Radio', an open-source playground for crafting antennas, tuning into hidden frequencies, and reclaiming the electromagnetic commons.

We design and document evolving ecosystems of DIY antennas, experimental receivers, and frequency-based interactions—enabling you to build, modify, repurpose, and share tools for sensing the invisible landscapes around us.

diyrrr empowers you to counteract technological opacity, planned obsolescence, and the erosion of our ability to listen beyond the algorithm.

By gaining hands-on knowledge, you will learn to construct and reconfigure the tools needed to hear, see, and interpret the world through waves and signals.

This knowledge expands in multiple directions, from radio-electronics and speculative design to environmental sensing and community-based media. diyrrr fosters a culture of self-made, modular devices that are easy to build, practical to use, and open to continuous evolution.
